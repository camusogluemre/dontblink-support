# Don't Blink — Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** July 18, 2026
**Developer:** Nikolaus Lemke ("we", "us")
**Contact:** niko.lemke@icloud.com

This policy describes what data the iOS game **Don't Blink** ("the app") handles, and how.

## The short version

- The app has **no accounts** and asks for **no personal information**.
- Your game progress **never leaves your device**.
- The only third-party data collection happens when **rewarded ads** are shown, by our advertising partner (LevelPlay / ironSource).
- If you decline the tracking permission, ads still work — they are simply not personalized.

## Data we do NOT collect

We do not collect, store or transmit: your name, email address, contacts, photos, location, microphone or camera data, or any account credentials. The app contains no chat, no user-generated content and no social features beyond Game Center leaderboards.

## Game progress (stored on your device only)

Best runs, coins, unlocks and settings are saved in a local file on your device. This data is never uploaded to us. Deleting the app permanently deletes it.

## Game Center (Apple)

If you are signed in to Game Center, the app submits your survival times and scores to Apple's leaderboards under your Game Center player identity. This is operated by Apple and covered by [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/). If you are not signed in, nothing is submitted.

## Advertising (LevelPlay / ironSource)

The app shows **optional rewarded video ads** (you choose to watch them in exchange for in-game coins). Ads are served by LevelPlay, an ironSource / Unity service. To deliver ads, measure their performance and prevent fraud, the ad SDK may collect:

- **Device identifiers** — the advertising identifier (IDFA), only if you grant the App Tracking Transparency permission; otherwise a non-persistent identifier
- **Advertising data** — which ads were shown and how they were interacted with
- **Technical data** — device model, OS version, IP address (which implies coarse location), and similar diagnostics

This collection is described in [Unity's Privacy Policy](https://unity.com/legal/privacy-policy) and the [ironSource Privacy Policy](https://developers.is.com/ironsource-mobile/air/ironsource-mobile-privacy-policy/).

### App Tracking Transparency

On first launch the app asks for the iOS tracking permission. **Declining it does not limit the game in any way** — ads simply become non-personalized. You can change your choice anytime in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

## Children

The app is rated 9+ and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children; the only data collection in the app is the advertising collection described above, which is operated under the ad partner's compliance framework.

## Your choices and rights

- **Reset ad personalization / withdraw consent:** iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking
- **Delete local game data:** delete the app
- **Data collected by the ad partner:** requests can be made under Unity's privacy policy linked above
- **Questions or requests:** email us at niko.lemke@icloud.com

Depending on where you live (e.g. EU/EEA — GDPR, California — CCPA), you may have rights to access, correct or delete personal data held by us or our partners. Since we hold none ourselves, such requests will typically concern the ad partner; we will help you direct them.

## Changes

If we change this policy (for example, when adding new features or SDKs), we will update this page and the effective date above.
