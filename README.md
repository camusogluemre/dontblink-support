# Don't Blink — Support

**Don't Blink** is a one-button horror-arcade runner for iOS. Your eyelids are the controller: tap to blink, survive the lab.

## FAQ

**How do I play?**
One tap = one blink. Keep your eyes open too long and the strain kills you. Blink at the wrong moment and the lab kills you. Each obstacle teaches its rule the first time you see it.

**My progress disappeared — where is it saved?**
Progress (best runs, coins, settings) is saved locally on your device. Deleting the app deletes your progress. Leaderboard scores stay on Game Center.

**Leaderboards don't show my score.**
Make sure you are signed in to Game Center (Settings → Game Center on your device). Scores submitted while offline are sent automatically the next time you open the game.

**The ads don't load.**
Rewarded ads (the optional "free coins" video) need a network connection. If an ad is not ready, the button simply won't award coins — try again later. Everything else in the game works fully offline.

**How do I report a bug?**
Email us or open an issue with: your device model, iOS version, and what happened. Screenshots or screen recordings help a lot.

## Privacy

Game progress never leaves your device. Rewarded ads are provided by LevelPlay (ironSource); see the App Store listing's privacy section for the data practices. The game asks for App Tracking Transparency permission — declining it only means ads are non-personalized.
